Introduction

Ramachandra PVT LTD is a leading retail company in India, with a large number of stores across the country. Each store has multiple firms, and all firms have a significant workforce spread across various locations. The company faced major challenges in managing employee attendance and ensuring accurate payroll calculations for its extensive workforce. The existing eSSL attendance system had limitations that led to operational inefficiencies and recurring payroll errors, necessitating a comprehensive solution to streamline these critical processes. Additionally, all users were restricted from accessing certain modules based on their permissions, and they faced difficulties in applying for leaves. Recognizing the need for improvement, Ramachandra PVT LTD sought a reliable and scalable solution to enhance their attendance management and payroll processing capabilities.

Background and Requirements

The client, Ramachandra PVT LTD, was already using the Zoho platform for various business operations. They requested a similar solution tailored to their attendance management and payroll processing needs, work on the project in a manner that aligned with their familiarity with the Zoho application.

Analysis and Proof of Concept

As the junior developer, I played a crucial role in analyzing the existing Zoho application, meticulously examining all the relevant components, modules, and features.Based on this comprehensive analysis, I created a proof of concept (POC) to demonstrate how we could replicate and enhance the desired functionalities within our proposed solution.

Advanced Filter

I will implement multi-criteria filtering to allow users to filter by multiple attributes (e.g., category, employee ID, department). The filtering options will include searching for data that contains, does not contain, matches exactly, or is similar to the input. This functionality will make it easier to search for and find specific data.

Monthly Attendance

I will intergrate a monthly attendance system to track who is coming to the organization or taking leave. This data will be used to calculate salaries and other related matters.

Technology Stack

The application was built using a modern technology stack, with React.js for the frontend, Django Rest Framework for the backend, PostgreSQL for the database, and Celery/Cronjob for automated processes.One unique technological aspect was the integration of Apollo GraphQL for real-time data handling, such as notifications and updates.
